General Steps to Perform Genetic Engineering

Step Name Description
1 DNA Extraction DNA is removed from an organism
2 Gene Cloning Scientists use gene cloning to multiple and isolate the desired gene
3 Gene Design The gene is edited to work in the target organism by adding or removing nucleotides
4 Gene Insertion The edited DNA is inserted back into an organism
